--- layout: post title: Simplify Media - Rockin' on the Road tags: - Disruptive Tech - Mac OS X - Solution - '' status: publish type: post published: true meta: {} --- As a system administrator with a very large music collection, I've always been mildly irritated at the difficulty accessing my "master" music library while away from home. Enter Simply Media a free, small application which does just as the name promises. My iTunes library back home just shows up in my shared iTunes listing, regardless of where I am. No firewall hackery, nothing to configure, it just works, and works well. The iTunes integration is fantastic.
