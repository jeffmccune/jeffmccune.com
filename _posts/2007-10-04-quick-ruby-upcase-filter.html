--- layout: post title: Quick Ruby Upcase Filter tags: - North Star Labs - Solution - System Administration - filter - pipe - ruby - uppercase status: publish type: post published: true meta: {} --- This is a real quick one. I noticed uuidgen under linux produces lower case strings, while it's Mac OS X counterpart produces uppercase strings. I generate UUID's for the certificate CN field in Puppet, so I wanted them to match. This one-liner did the trick nicely. 
uuidgen | ruby -pe '$_.upcase!'
